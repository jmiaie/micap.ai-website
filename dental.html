The Real Problem

Why Dental Practices Lose New Patients Every Day

These aren't operational inefficiencies — they're revenue leaks you can quantify and fix.

📞
Lunch Hour & After-Hours Calls
Most new patient calls come during lunch (12–2pm) and after 5pm — exactly when your front desk is unavailable. These are your highest-value leads, and they're going to voicemail.
🦷
New Patient Lifetime Value Is Massive
A missed new patient call isn't just one appointment — it's $1,200–$2,400 in lifetime value. Missing 5 calls per week costs your practice $300K–$600K over 5 years.
📅
Scheduling Friction Kills Conversions
Even patients who reach you drop off when scheduling requires a callback or long hold times. Every friction point costs you 15–25% of potential bookings.

No Reactivation System
Inactive patients who haven't visited in 12+ months represent your highest-ROI growth opportunity. Without an automated reactivation system, they simply drift to competitors.

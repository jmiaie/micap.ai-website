Knowledge Base

Your Questions, Answered

Find answers to the most common questions about working with Micap AI and deploying digital employees in your business.

What are Digital Employees?

+

Digital Employees are AI-powered autonomous agents designed to handle specific business processes and tasks. Unlike traditional software, they learn from data, adapt to changes, and make intelligent decisions. Our 10 Digital Employees cover core business functions including customer service, data analysis, content creation, lead qualification, and more. They work 24/7 without breaks, reducing operational costs by 60-80% while improving efficiency and accuracy.

How much can we save with AI automation?

+

Savings depend on your current operations, but most businesses see 40-70% reduction in labor costs for automated processes. Use our ROI Calculator to estimate your specific savings. For example, a company with 5 employees spending 30% of their time on repetitive tasks could save $150,000-$300,000 annually. Our Digital Employees typically pay for themselves within 3-6 months.

How long does implementation take?

+

Most Digital Employee deployments take 2-4 weeks from contract signing to go-live. Complex integrations with legacy systems may take 6-8 weeks. We handle the entire process: assessment, configuration, integration, testing, and training. You'll see results within the first month, with full optimization by month 3.

Is AI automation secure for sensitive data?

+

Yes. Our Digital Employees operate within your private infrastructure with enterprise-grade encryption. We comply with GDPR, HIPAA, and SOC 2 standards. All data stays within your systems - we never access or store your business information. We implement role-based access controls, audit logging, and regular security assessments. Your data security is our top priority.

What if the AI makes a mistake?

+

Our Digital Employees are designed with built-in safeguards. Critical decisions require human approval. We implement confidence thresholds - if the AI is unsure, it escalates to your team. All actions are logged and auditable. During the first 30 days, we monitor performance closely and adjust parameters. Most errors are caught and corrected automatically, with a human-in-the-loop for edge cases.

Can we integrate with our existing systems?

+

Absolutely. Our Digital Employees integrate with most business systems: CRM (Salesforce, HubSpot), ERP (SAP, NetSuite), communication tools (Slack, Teams), databases, APIs, and custom applications. We handle the technical integration so you don't have to. If your system has an API, we can connect it. Custom integrations are available for legacy systems.

No. We handle all technical implementation. Your team just needs to provide business requirements and process documentation.

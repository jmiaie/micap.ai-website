Why Plumbing Companies Lose Emergency Revenue

  • After-Hours Call Abandonment: 40-50% of emergency calls happen outside business hours. They go to voicemail. Customers call a competitor.
  • Dispatch Bottleneck: During peak emergencies (freezing weather, holiday weekends), dispatch gets overwhelmed. Calls wait on hold. Customers hang up.
  • Slow Scheduling: Even when you capture the call, scheduling is manual. Customers wait for callbacks. By then, they've hired someone else.
  • No Follow-Up System: Leads who don't convert immediately rarely get follow-up. You lose them permanently.

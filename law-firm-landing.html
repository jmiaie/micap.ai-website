Why Law Firms Lose Leads

  • Unanswered Calls During Business Hours: Attorneys are in meetings. Intake staff are overwhelmed. Calls go to voicemail. Potential clients call competitors.
  • After-Hours Call Abandonment: Legal problems don't follow business hours. 50-70% of after-hours calls are lost to voicemail.
  • Slow, Manual Intake Process: Even when you capture the call, intake is slow. Clients have to wait for callbacks. By then, they've hired another firm.
  • Poor Lead Qualification: Without AI qualification, intake staff waste time on unqualified leads while missing qualified ones.
  • No Follow-Up System: Leads who don't convert on first contact rarely get follow-up. You lose 40-50% of interested leads.

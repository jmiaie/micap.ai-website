Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2, 2026

Summary: We collect minimal personal data only when you voluntarily provide it through forms or tools. We never sell your data. We use it only to provide services and communicate with you. You can request deletion anytime.

1. Introduction

Micap AI ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") operates the micap-ai.manus.space website (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our Site and use our services.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Site.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Fill out contact forms or inquiry forms
  • Use our ROI Calculator or AI Readiness Assessment
  • Subscribe to our newsletter
  • Request information about our services
  • Send us emails or messages

This information may include: name, email address, phone number, company name, job title, and any other information you choose to provide.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our Site, we automatically collect certain information about your device and how you interact with our Site:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website
  • Device type and screen resolution
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

2.3 Cookies and Tracking

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use our Site. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect information about your browsing behavior. Your IP address is anonymized. You can opt-out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • To respond to your inquiries and requests
  • To send you marketing communications (only if you opt-in)
  • To analyze Site usage and improve user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To prevent fraud and enhance security
  • To generate leads and follow up on inquiries

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Period.

We may share your information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our Site and conducting our business, including:

These service providers are contractually obligated to use your information only as necessary to provide services to us.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or if we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to:

  • Protect against fraud or security threats

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at privacy@micap-ai.manus.space.

6.1 Access and Portability

You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you and to receive a copy in a portable format.

You have the right to request correction of inaccurate information or deletion of your personal data. We will comply with such requests within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

You can opt-out of marketing communications by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any email we send, or by contacting us directly.

6.4 Do Not Track

Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" feature. Our Site does not currently respond to Do Not Track signals, but you can control tracking through your browser settings and opt-out tools.

7. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
